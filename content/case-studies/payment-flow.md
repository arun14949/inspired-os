# Payment Flow Optimization

## Overview
Redesigned the payment experience to reduce friction and increase conversion rates.

## Problem
High drop-off rates during the payment process were resulting in lost revenue and frustrated users.

## Solution
Simplified the payment flow by reducing steps, adding progress indicators, and implementing smart defaults.

## Process
1. Mapped the existing payment funnel with analytics
2. Identified high-friction points through user research
3. Redesigned the flow with fewer steps
4. Added trust signals and clear pricing
5. Tested with users and iterated

## Impact
- Reduced payment drop-off
- Increased conversion rate
- Improved user confidence in the payment process

## Technologies
- UX Research
- Interaction Design
- Funnel Analytics
